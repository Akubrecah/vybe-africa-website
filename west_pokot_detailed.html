ELEVATING YOUTH VOICES

Empowering the next generation of changemakers in West Pokot through innovation, advocacy, and action.

Our Story in West Pokot

Born in the heart of West Pokot, VYBE Africa was established by local youth who saw the untapped potential of their peers. We recognized that true community transformation requires those who live the reality to be the architects of change.

Our unique approach centers on grassroots empowerment, ensuring that young people are not just beneficiaries, but active decision-makers. We envision a West Pokot where every young person is equipped to advocate for their rights, protect their environment, and shape their governance.
