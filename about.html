Voices of Youth for Better Engagement Africa

Our Story

VYBE Africa is a youth-led Community-Based Organization (CBO) located in West Pokot County, Kenya. Our name stands for Voices of Youth for Better Engagement. We were founded on the belief that young people are not just beneficiaries of development, but active architects of their own future.


Driven by the tagline "Dream. Dare. Do.", we work tirelessly to address critical challenges facing our community, from Sexual and Reproductive Health rights to Climate Action and Inclusive Governance.

Our Vision

A society where youth are empowered, engaged, and leading sustainable development in West Pokot and beyond.

Our Mission

To amplify the voices of youth, promote health and well-being, protect children, and foster inclusive governance through advocacy, eduction, and community action.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We operate with transparency and honesty in all our engagements.

Inclusivity

We believe in leaving no one behind, amplifying marginalized voices.

Innovation

We dare to dream and implement creative solutions to local problems.
