The Spark

Our Origins in West Pokot

VYBE Africa began as a small, passionate group of young individuals in West Pokot County, Kenya. We saw firsthand the challenges our peers faced and decided it was time to change the narrative.

Founded on the belief that young people are active architects of their own future, we started by tackling the most immediate issues: Sexual and Reproductive Health rights and access to education. Our early days were defined by grassroots organizing, listening to community needs, and building trust.

The Movement

Growing to a Regional CBO

What started as a local initiative quickly gained momentum. Driven by our tagline 'Dream. Dare. Do.', our programs expanded to encompass Climate Action and Inclusive Governance.

Today, VYBE Africa is a recognized regional Community-Based Organization. We empower thousands of youths across the region to advocate for their rights, engage in sustainable practices, and demand accountable leadership. We are not just a charity; we are a movement for systemic change.
